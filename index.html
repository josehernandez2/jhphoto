About

Hello! My name is Jose Hernandez II and welcome to my online portfolio. I've been a practicing freelance photographer in the Eastern Nebraska area for about half a decade now. I've studied photo composition under Ben Long's courses on Lynda.com, where I learned how to work with a number of compositional elements and rules. I have also studied photo processing techniques from Timothy Sexton and Chris Orwig. You can view some of the credentials I received through Linkedin Learning and Lynda.com on my Linkedin Profile. I shoot with a Canon 6d and process my photos with Photoshop. I have also been studying web development through Coursera and The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, which is why I also designed and developed this website. Feel free to email or call me, or you can head over to the contact page and send me a message through there.
